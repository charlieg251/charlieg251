Final Prohect

This program is designed to teach Spanish vocabulary. It provides scenarios based on shopping at a bookstore that the player must choose the correct answer to. Three boxes with three different answers are provided for each scenario. Choosing the wrong answer will show an error screen and one point will be deducted from your score. Choosing the correct answer will give you one point and you will proceed to the next scenario. You can earn a maximum of 10 points- try to get as many as you can!
